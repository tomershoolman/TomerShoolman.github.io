My filmmaking journey began in high school when I took a film major. There I first discovered my profound passion for storytelling while focusing on making documentary films. Since then, in my free time, I've been working on directing and editing movies.

These days, I have a passion for creating short films, spanning a range of genres from brand films to after movies, university projects, and more. Each short film is a unique venture into storytelling, where I can experiment with different techniques, styles, and perspectives

To our YouTube channel

My passion for making nature content, mainly films, began with an inspiring trip to Tanzania. Witnessing the wild landscapes and animals up close was nothing short of amazing, igniting a desire within me to capture and share those incredible experiences

Since then, I've taken my camera to the enchanting landscapes of nature, embarking on adventures that range from diving trips in the crystal-clear waters of the Coral Triangle in Eilat to hiking the Israel National Trail , where the rich tapestry of history blends seamlessly with the natural beauty. My journeys have also taken me to the dramatic landscapes of Iceland, where volcanoes, glaciers, and hot springs coexist in an otherworldly mix.
